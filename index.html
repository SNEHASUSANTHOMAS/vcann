Who we are

The Vibrant Community Action Network (VCAN) is an actualized aspiration for a bunch of passionate young professionals with specializations in development studies, public policy, local governance, social engineering, gender studies, social work, and various social science disciplines from renowned universities of India, including the Rajiv Gandhi National Institute of Youth Development (considered being the only youth development institute in South Asia), who are keen to bring systematic change at the grassroots, engaging with government representatives at constituency level. VCAN facilitates the fruition of youth's potential by portraying them as active citizens to work for development and their realization as Social Change Makers. Considered to be an active agent of the initiative, ‘Youth for Global Goals (Youth4GG)’, the young development practitioners of VCAN, reach out to the grassroots to enable a community-led approach to enhance local capacity, by encouraging wider stakeholder participation. Through, VCAN we are intended to frame and strategize inclusive models to translate the existing society by focusing on all aspects of development.

We are unique

Working within the system of governance, ensuring good governance for inclusive development, championing participation at the grassroots, VCAN, by dint of a professional approach, bourn and act to level up the society in virtue of decentralized sustainable development.

Our Model

Development- the multi-dimensional concept cannot be denied to anyone. Inclusive development should be fostered through framing relevant and adequate policies and programmes. VCAN is intended to frame and strategize inclusive models to translate the existing society into a novel realm by focusing on all aspects of development. We are here for reconstructing the systemic framework which is currently distracting the democratic fabric of the territory, promoting good governance by endorsing better actions. VCAN upholds Constituency Management, Community-led Programmes, and Youth-led Programmes.
